## ğŸ› ï¸ Technology & Our Privacy Promise

While this repository does not contain source code, we believe in transparency about how our app is built and how we protect your data.

### Our Privacy Promise

- **Offline by Default:** Your entries are stored on your device, not our servers, unless you opt-in to cloud sync.
- **Zero-Knowledge Encryption:** When you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted with a key that we never see. We cannot read your journal entries.
- **No Tracking, No Ads:** We do not use third-party analytics trackers or sell your data. Our business model is a simple, optional Pro subscription.

### Built With Modern Tools

Aura Diary is built with a modern, secure, and scalable tech stack to ensure a high-quality experience.

- **Frontend:** React (Vite) & TypeScript
- **Backend & Database:** Supabase
- **On-Device AI:** Whisper.cpp & Optimized SLMs
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
